Audience Issues & Approach

Client Information and Objectives
Client Name and Decision Maker Dennis Bateman
Business Name Pacific Northwest Gold Buyers
Redesign or New Website This is a New Website
Type of Website This is a Ecommerce website with a fully functioning "Appraisal Kit" form that enables the user to order an "Appraisal Kit" using the interface I developed. This form then goes to an SQL database hosted by Hostgator that my client can use to access his submission requests.
Clients Goals My client would like a Ecommerce website thats fully capable of purchasing gold from his customers. It's important for his customers to be able to easily navigate throughout his site, in and out of pages using the navigation menu when appropriate. He would also like the "Appraisal Kit" form to be located on the majority of his site pages.

Segmented Audiences
Audiences Issues, Concerns, Needs Approach

Collectors

By segmenting investors and collectors of gold and silver coins, we were able to generate quantitative and qualitative data, which I was then able to use to optimize Mr.Batemans website for performance and user accessibility.

    Issues, Concerns, Needs

  • Are there enough links? Are there too many?
  • Where do you want your links to go?
  • Where do you want your form to be?
  • Do you want multiple forms?

    Approach

  • Form
  • Shopping Cart
  • Credit Card Form
  • Social Media Links
  • Live Ticker
  • Bit Coin Converter

Loan Equity

Developing a strategy to market this website to people that are looking for a loan, the same way a bank would, I believe will attract the right people looking for this service. Those who work 8 to 10 hour work days who are still having trouble paying their bills may think this service is helpful. Looking back on better financial times would give them a chance to turn some of their jewelry into cash that can be used today.
  • Does the Site explain your process?
  • Is the Apprasial process explained throughly?
  • Do you want your terms and conditions on each page?
  • Does the form allow you to generate enough data to make an accurate appraisal?

    Approach

  • Credit Card Form
  • Accepting Bit Coin
  • Investment/Loan Options
  • Trading Gold as Stock
  • Wall Street

Retired Senior Adults

Marketing this website to those that are elderly in age is a demographic that will be looking for this service in order to sell their gold for cash. Adults 65 years of age and up have shown to use the internet about 1.5 times more,(when purchasing goods) then those adults in their 40's,"The largest proportion of people buying online once or twice is found among those aged 55-74 (39 %)"-EUROPEAN STATISTICS, https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/. Also those 65 and up are using the internet more and more each year as they become accustomed to it.
  • Is there enough information for the elderly?
  • Is this site optimized for accessibility?
  • Is the interface UI/UX and the user experience optimized for the elderly?
  • Are the menus and words large?

    Approach

  • Design UI with large font sizes.
  • Make site easy to navigate
  • Are there multiple links taking user to home page?
  • Are there multiple in-site links that will take user to "form"?
